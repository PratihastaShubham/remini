The Vision

Pokémon Reminiscencia isn't just another fan game. It transforms the classic formula into a Roguelike adventure. No gyms, no grinding, just pure strategy and a deep, emotional story.

Built on the Pokémon Essentials engine, it features custom pixel art and a unique "Heart Scale" system that lets you focus on building your team rather than leveling it up.

Why Play Reminiscencia?

Story Driven

A 15+ hour narrative following Anthony and Phione across distant, atmospheric islands.

True Strategy

Every Pokémon is set to Level 50. Victory depends on your choices, not your level.

Complete Pokedex

Includes all officially released Pokémon with high-quality custom animations.

Modern Features

Multiple modes including Story, DLC sequel (Johto), and an endless Replayable Mode.
